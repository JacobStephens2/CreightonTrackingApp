Creighton Cycle Tracker is a simple, private app for tracking your fertility cycle using the Creighton Model System (CrMS).
Record your daily observations including bleeding level, mucus characteristics, stretch, and frequency. The app automatically computes your CrMS stamps — green for dry days, red for bleeding, white for fertile mucus, and yellow for days of basic infertile pattern — with automatic peak day detection and post-peak counting.
View your cycle data the way it's meant to be seen with the classic 35-column Creighton chart, just like the paper charts used by FertilityCare Practitioners. Switch to the calendar view for a monthly overview with color-coded stamps at a glance.
Share your chart with your FertilityCare Practitioner using a secure, read-only link. No account needed on their end — just send the link and they can review your chart from any device.
Your data stays on your device by default. The app works fully offline with no internet required. Create an optional account to back up your data and sync across devices.
Export your data anytime as a JSON backup or CSV chart export. Your data is yours.
Key features:
- Daily observation entry with bleeding, mucus, stretch, frequency, and notes
- Auto-computed CrMS stamps with peak day and post-peak count
- Classic 35-column Creighton chart view
- Monthly calendar view with color-coded stamps
- Automatic cycle detection
- Provider sharing with secure read-only links
- Full offline support
- Optional cloud sync across devices
- Data export in JSON and CSV formats
- No ads, no tracking, no subscriptions
